Spirit Animal

I believe my spirit animal is a sloth, not because I think I'm lazy, but because I believe that these wonderous creatures reflect my laid back nature. I believe that in this life, we don't need to worry about things we can't control, instead we need to constantly immerse ourselves in the present, and in this way, enjoy the fullness of the human experience.

Spirit animals are sort of like a special friend in the spirit realm, a companion who watches over us and guides us along our journey through life.

Fun Fact: Despite their slow-moving nature, sloths are actually excellent swimmers! They can even hold their breath for up to 40 minutes underwater!
